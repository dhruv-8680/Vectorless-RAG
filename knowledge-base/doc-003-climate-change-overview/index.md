- **chunk-003.md** | Keywords: temperature rise, Arctic amplification, climate models, ice-albedo feedback, 1.5 degrees | "Global average temperature has risen 1.1°C above pre-industrial levels, with Arctic regions warming 2-3 times faster, and projections suggest 2.5-4.5°C rise by 2100 without significant emissions reductions."

## Ocean Impacts
- **chunk-004.md** | Keywords: ocean warming, sea level rise, ocean acidification, coral bleaching, marine ecosystems | "Oceans have absorbed 90% of excess heat, causing coral bleaching, 20 cm of sea level rise since 1900, and a 30% increase in acidity that threatens shell-forming organisms."

## Extreme Weather Events
- **chunk-005.md** | Keywords: extreme weather, heatwaves, wildfires, tropical cyclones, flooding, drought | "Climate change intensifies extreme weather including heavier precipitation, more frequent heatwaves, stronger tropical cyclones, and more severe wildfires through warmer air holding more moisture and increased evaporation."

## Impacts on Biodiversity
- **chunk-006.md** | Keywords: biodiversity, species extinction, coral reefs, phenological mismatch, habitat migration, IPCC | "Climate change threatens biodiversity through habitat shifts, phenological mismatches, and extinction risk — the IPCC estimates 18% of land species at high risk at 2°C warming and over 50% at 4.5°C."

## Mitigation Strategies
- **chunk-007.md** | Keywords: renewable energy, electric vehicles, carbon capture, energy efficiency, methane reduction, solar wind | "Key mitigation strategies include transitioning to renewable energy (solar costs down 89% since 2010), electrifying transportation, improving energy efficiency, deploying carbon capture technologies, and reducing methane emissions."

## Adaptation Strategies
- **chunk-008.md** | Keywords: adaptation, infrastructure resilience, agricultural adaptation, early warning systems, water management, ecosystem-based adaptation | "Adaptation strategies for unavoidable climate impacts include building resilient infrastructure, developing drought-resistant crops, improving early warning systems, restoring protective ecosystems, and enhancing water management."
